Pipeline

From raw traffic records to model artifacts

The project maps attacks into DoS, Probe, R2L, U2R, and Normal classes; one-hot encodes protocol, service, and flag fields; scales all encoded features; and evaluates multiple learning approaches against the full NSL-KDD test set.

  1. 01 Load NSL-KDD train and test files

    125,973 train records and 22,544 test records with 41 raw traffic features.

  2. 02 Encode and scale

    Categorical fields become one-hot columns, producing 122 model-ready features.

  3. 03 Train and compare

    XGBoost cross-validation, final XGBoost, CNN-LSTM, autoencoder, and averaged ensemble outputs are evaluated.

  4. 04 Deploy free static app

    The web app uses static assets only, keeping hosting compatible with free tiers and no paid inference services.
